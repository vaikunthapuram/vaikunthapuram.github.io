DIVINE NAMES OF NAARAAYANA

LORD AS VISHVAM

The entire universe is the manifestation of the Divine Lord. He is the sole cause of the universe and is never separated from us, and whatever we see is nothing but the expansion of his Self. Everything around us is his universal body
and loving the universe and taking care of the universe is like anointing the body of the Lord. At the beginning of each cycle, Lord Bramha sitting on Lotus emerged from the navel of Garbhodakshayi Vishnu.This play of Lord that indicates that he is the sole cause of the entire universe is codified here in the first divine name of the sahasranaama.

LORD AS VISHNU

Lord pervades all the animate and inanimate objects of the universe. In his incarnation as Lord Vaamana, he assumed a massive form and measured the entire earth,heaven, and beyond with his three steps.
This wondrous play of Lord indicates his all-pervasive nature, acknowledging his presence in every animate and inanimate objects in the cosmos,not limited by time, space, or susbstance.

LORD AS VASHATKAAR

Vashat is a sacred sound used in pouring offerings while performing sacred fire rituals. This divine name uplifts us to perform sacrifices and contribute our time, efforts, and skills for the betterment of others in the society.
The following words of great poet, Rabindranath Tagore:"The one who plants trees, knowing that he will never sit in their shade, has at least started to understand the meaning of life."encapsulates in them the essence of
divine name: "Vashatkaarah" as the collective sacrifices made by any society becomes the collective resources available for the society and this divine name reminds each one of us to contribute whenever and wherever we can.

LORD AS BHOOT-BHAVYA-BHAVAT-PRABHUH

The meaning of "Bhoot-bhavya-bhavat-prabhuh" is Lord of past, present, and future. Since Lord Vishnu transcends the states of time and is always aware of these states, he is called as Bhoot-bhavya-bhavat-prabhuh.
