The Heartfelt Journey of Creating This Website!

This website represents the culmination of discussions between two spiritual companions
who fell in love with the names of Lord; one engages in speaking about the Lord while the other
excels in inquiring and listening with great attentiveness. We graciously present our reflections
at the lotus feet of our esteemed spiritual master, Om Swami, and dedicate this endeavor to all
those who harbora deep affection for the divine name and narratives of the Lord.
